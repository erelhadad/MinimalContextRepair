Sydney
Sydney is the capital city of Australia and the state capital of New South Wales. It has over 4 million inhabitants and is built on hills surrounding Sydney Harbour, where the iconic Sydney Opera House and Harbour Bridge are located. Sydney has a temperate climate and is one of the most multicultural cities in the world, known for its beaches, Chinatown, Darling Harbour, and extensive public transportation system that includes trains, buses, and ferries.
