Wiz Khalifa Seems to Reveal He Thinks
Earth Is Flat 'Because I've Traveled So
Much': 'Not Possible to Go Up and
Down'
In a new episode of 'The Joe Budden Podcast,' the rapper also questioned space
exploration
In an exclusive Patreon episode of The Joe Budden Podcast released on
Monday, April 21, the rapper opened up about his apparent thoughts on
Earth's shape despite it being proven to be round more than 2,000 years
ago, per Billboard.
"Nah, it’s not that. I think that there’s more masses than just what we see
because it was one thing before and it spread out," he said, appearing to
refer to the supercontinent of Pangaea.
“Because when I travel, the routes that we take and how we do it, it’s not
possible to go up and down. You’re just going straight. That’s the only
reason I think that," said the "Black and Yellow" performer.
Khalifa then pivoted by sharing his skepticism of space exploration.
"I don’t believe in space exploration at all,” he said. “I don’t believe that
they explore space as much as they say that they do.”
